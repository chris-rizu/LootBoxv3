Our Story

LootBox was born from a passion for PC gaming and a frustration with limited options in the market. Founded by enthusiasts who understood the thrill of building the perfect rig, we set out to create a platform where quality meets accessibility. What started as a small venture has grown into a trusted destination for thousands of gamers.

What We Do

We curate and deliver premium PC components from world-leading brands. From cutting-edge graphics cards and processors to precision gaming peripherals, we provide everything you need to build, upgrade, or enhance your setup. Our expert team tests and verifies every product to ensure peak performance.

Our Mission

To empower gamers and creators with access to premium hardware at competitive prices, backed by exceptional customer service and expert guidance. We believe everyone deserves a high-performance PC experience.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted PC hardware destination in Southeast Asia, known for authenticity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to the gaming community. We aim to shape the future of PC building.
